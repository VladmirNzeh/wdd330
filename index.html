Smarter Land Decisions

LandView is a modern web app that helps you find, compare, and analyze land with detailed, interactive tools. From satellite views to zoning overlays and ownership details, it's built to empower developers, homebuyers, and urban planners.

What You Can Do

  • Create an Account and Save Favorite Spots
  • Smart Search with Filters like Zoning and Elevation
  • Interactive Maps with Real-Time Overlays
  • Detailed Property Insights
  • Compare Listings Side by Side
  • Generate Reports and Share Links

Who It's For

Primary Users

  • Real Estate Developers
  • Future Homeowners
  • Land Use Researchers

Secondary Users

  • Real Estate Agents
  • Urban Planners & Students

Tech & Tools

LandView leverages ArcGIS and Google Maps APIs for real-time maps, overlays, and directions. User preferences are saved using localStorage and Firebase for account-linked data.
